Marvel Comics Database

A database for cataloging characters, events, locations, superpowers, and teams of the Marvel Comics universe.

  • Characters - Add, update, and delete characters. Search characters by codename, or filter them by their home.
  • Events - Add, update, and delete events. Search events by name, or filter them by their start year.
  • Locations - Add, update, and delete locations. Search locations by name.
  • Teams - Add, update, and delete teams. Search teams by name.
  • Superpowers - Add, update, and delete superpowers. Search superpowers by description.
  • Character Superpowers - Add and delete superpowers for each character.
